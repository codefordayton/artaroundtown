About Art Around Town

Art Around Town Dayton is a community-powered calendar and directory for the local arts scene. It carries forward the work of Essential Arts Dayton — connecting artists, galleries, and art lovers in one place. Anyone can browse events; artists and venues can create a free account to submit and manage their listings.
